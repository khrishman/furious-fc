Match Recap: Furious FC 3 - 1 Storm United

Furious FC secured a commanding 3-1 victory over rivals Storm United last night. Goals from Kane (2) and Silva (1) sealed the deal in front of 40,000 roaring fans.

The first half saw dominance by Furious with 68% possession. The highlight was a long-range stunner by Silva in the 34th minute.

With this win, Furious FC climbs to 2nd in the league standings.
