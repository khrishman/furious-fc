Official: Furious FC Signs Trent Alexander-Arnold

Furious FC is thrilled to announce the signing of 26-year-old Right-back from England.Trent Alexander-Arnold from liverpool. Known for his good defendiong skill as well and good offensive quality, Trent will wear jersey number 12.

Manager Udip Maharjan said, "Trent will bring strong energy and defensive skills to our defensive at the same time he will also be great offensively. We’re building for the future."

The deal is rumored to be worth €10 million on a 4-year contract.
